About Us

Over the years, there have been countless websites, forums, apps, etc. dedicated to providing "reviews" of restaurants and their staff.
We have decided to create a private community where restaurant staff have a place to rant about their customers and provide honest reviews in a manner that won't get them fired.

An Example of What We Are:

Dear Customer Who Decided They Needed 7 Lemons for Their Water...
The request itself was weird enough because no human on this planet needs 7 Lemons for their glass of water. Then you started adding sugar to your "water". I should have charged you for a lemonade but apparently that's against company policy.
Sincerely,
A Waitress Losing Her Patience
